# 🎯 Socratic AI — OKR (Quarter 1)

## 🧠 Objective
Help users experience their first “aha moment” with Socratic AI—shifting from asking for answers to thinking clearly.

---

## 📊 Key Results

### KR1 — Leading (User Behavior)
**40%** new users complete at least **1 full Socratic session** (≥3 back-and-forth questions) in their first visit

---

### KR2 — Lagging (Business)
Reach **1,000 MAU** with at least **5% converting to paid** (or waitlist-to-paid intent)

---

### KR3 — Quality (Retention)
Achieve **30% Week-1 retention** and **NPS ≥ 40**

---

## 🔥 Notes (for pitch alignment)

- KR1 → đo “user có thực sự dùng đúng cách không”
- KR2 → chứng minh có demand thật
- KR3 → validate product có tạo value hay không
